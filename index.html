Red Leaf Fine Landscapes is a unique landscape design and build company operating in the Greater Toronto Area. We offer a wide variety of services that can be custom tailored to every budget.

As a professional member of Landscape Ontario, Red Leaf Fine Landscapes takes pride in meeting the high standards of this respected horticultural trades organization. Landscape Ontario continues to raise the bar for the landscaping industry by requiring its members to observe its principles of ethical and professional conduct. Members also have access to the latest technical information, resources and services. Customers using a Landscape Ontario contractor are assured the peace of mind they deserve.

Our in-depth planning and highly professional execution creates artistic outdoor environments that will make any homeowner proud. Every team member of Red Leaf Fine Landscapes takes pride in the workmanship we provide to our clients, guaranteeing a high quality product.

Custom Designs

Visualize yourself in your future garden by seeing a computerized 3D model, tailored to your lifestyle and needs.

Stone Work

Natural Stone has a timeless style and elegance, and is the foundation of beautiful landscaping. Enhance your garden with a stone walkway, steps or perhaps a wall or patio.

Custom Carpentry

Decks, fences, pergolas and sheds, tailored to your sense of style, provide practical and visual advantages, such as shade, shelter, utility and privacy.

Planting Plans

Red Leaf Fine Landscapes takes all the characteristics of your property into consideration to establish detailed planting plans that integrate robust plant life, colour, size and texture to provide a beautiful aesthetic appearance.

Landscape Lighting

Extend your outdoor enjoyment into the evening and night time with accent lighting to illuminate walks, landscape features and entertainment spaces.

Large Tree Installation

Red Leaf Fine Landscapes has access to numerous species of mature trees that can grace your garden.

It starts with a meeting. We discuss your vision and requests for the project. The site is photographed, measurements are taken, and all information is brought back to our studio. At this point, we transcribe the details into a design, tailored to your requirements and style.

Once completed, we walk you through a 3D model of your future garden, using design and building plans as reference. Revisions, if necessary, are made. When the design is finalized and agreed upon, the start date is scheduled.

During the times when we are not at your premises, orders are being placed with suppliers, the permit application process is underway, and the building plans are being reviewed amongst the crew. The scheduled start date arrives, and construction begins.

Throughout the project, we pay particular attention to quality standards and to your comfort. We always welcome your questions while we build your future garden — after all, your satisfaction is the final product. When completed, we take time to discuss the intricacies of your new garden, and address any follow-up questions.
